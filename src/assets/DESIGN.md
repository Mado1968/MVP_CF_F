# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core visual theme of our design system, reflecting our brand identity and user experience principles.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is carefully selected to ensure consistency, accessibility, and brand recognition across all interfaces.

### Theme Colors
- **Primary Color:** `#1F4AA8` - This is our brand's most distinctive color, used for key interactive elements, call-to-actions, and primary branding.
- **Secondary Color:** `#2EC4B6` - A supporting color used for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions, providing visual depth.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#16315F` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements, adding subtle emphasis.
- **Neutral Color:** `#F7F9FC` - Our base color for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, ensuring a clean and legible foundation.

The overall color mode is set to `light`, providing a bright and airy default experience.

## Typography

Typography plays a crucial role in readability and information hierarchy. We utilize a distinct set of fonts for different text roles.

- **Headline Font:** `manrope` - Chosen for its modern yet readable qualities, ideal for prominent headings and titles.
- **Body Font:** `plusJakartaSans` - Selected for its excellent legibility at smaller sizes, suitable for all general body text.
- **Label Font:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for form labels, buttons, and other interactive elements, ensuring clarity and consistency with body text.

## Shape and Form

The `roundedness` of UI elements contributes significantly to the overall feel of our design.
- Our UI elements feature a `roundedness` level of `3`, giving them a maximum, pill-shaped appearance for a soft and friendly aesthetic.

## Spacing

`spacing` defines the breathing room between and within UI elements, impacting visual hierarchy and comfort.
- We maintain a `spacing` level of `2` (Normal), providing a balanced and comfortable amount of whitespace throughout the interface.
